Hi, I'm Jenna Bloom

I'm a young storyteller and videographer studying journalism at the University of Maryland. I want to create investigative documentaries that cause people to rethink the way they look at our world.

I work as a layout designer and a video reporter for my university's independent student newspaper, The Diamondback. On the layout team, I craft pages for our print tabloids using InDesign. When I'm not doing journalism, I enjoy playing tennis or frisbee, watching PBS Frontline documentaries, and spending as much time with my dog as I can.
